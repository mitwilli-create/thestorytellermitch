The full reel runs 4 minutes 19 seconds, which is right for a screening and wrong for a forward. This is the meeting cut: 81 seconds, built to answer exactly one question a buyer actually asks: is that really the same voice in every language?

What you are watching

The two product beats (the brand-name line and the pour) from each of the seven finished market masters, back to back: Spanish master, then English, French, Portuguese, Italian, German, Dutch. The shots are identical on purpose. When the film cannot change, the only thing left for your ear to compare is the voice, while the captions and the market music bed shift under it. The cut is a pure re-edit of the shipped masters: no new generation, so what you hear is exactly what the full spots ship with.

What we checked before claiming it

Voice-holding-across-languages is the load-bearing claim of the whole bundle, so it went through the same blind protocol as the mixes: a multimodal reviewer scored the actual files with zero context, as a multilingual dubbing director deciding whether a paying client ships internationally. Three independent reviews (this clip, the full reel, the French compliance cut) and the scores are published, not summarized away:

ClaimBlind verdict
Same voice across all seven languagesHolds: 7/10 in all three independent reviews. "The core vocal timbre is distinctly the same person."

That last row is the honest ceiling, and it comes with a priced fix: a retake regenerates only the weak language's voice track, about 0.04 USD per market, gated by a native-speaker read-through before any paid-media buy. The full reports are in the bundle's receipts folder, alongside the rest of the QA no-ship trail.

How to use it

  • Forward it. 81 seconds survives a phone screen and a busy inbox; the reel does not. Send this first, the full kit after the meeting.
  • Play it with sound in the room. Ask the buyer to close their eyes at the language cards. The question answers itself.
  • When they ask about cost: each of those language versions cost about 0.93 USD on top of the master. The line-by-line proof is the receipts CSV.
