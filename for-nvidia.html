The first 90 days

  • Month 1 · Absorb

    A living voice-reference doc for each principal. Built by shadowing one high-stakes deliverable each: diction, argument logic, what they cut in rehearsal. The same way I built voice tooling and a banned-phrase checklist at Google.

  • Month 2 · Prove

    One keynote, brief to final approved script, on deadline. The proof it works: fewer structural edits than the last comparable speech, and the exec says it sounds like them before rehearsal, not after three rounds.

  • Month 2 · Make it legible

    A one-pager on the AI-drafting workflow. Which tools accelerate which stages, and where human judgment is non-negotiable because a leader's name is on the output. Shared infrastructure, not a black box.

  • Month 3 · Systematize

    A repeatable onboarding kit any future writer can use day one. The voice-reference docs, the pipeline map, and the workflow, so the system outlasts any single contributor, including me.
