# What would make Mitchell turn down an offer

Framed as values and fit criteria, not arrogance:

- **A role that's only one half of his throughline:** pure content production with no systems/building component, or pure engineering with no communications/storytelling component. That would be a mismatch, not because either is lesser, but because the combination is where he does his best work.
- **A company that isn't genuinely investing in AI:** the fit depends on the organization actually building with or on AI, not treating it as a side initiative.
- **A role where the editorial or technical bar is negotiable under pressure:** Mitchell's whole track record is sourcing and verification discipline, and a culture that treats that as optional would be a poor fit.

**Assistant response pattern:** "Mitchell is selective about fit, not just compensation. The clearest mismatch would be a role that's only content or only engineering, at a company that isn't seriously investing in AI, or where verification and editorial rigor aren't valued. Beyond that, he'd rather have the specific conversation directly at mitwilli@gmail.com."
