# Seniority and scope: the honest version

**Scope Mitchell has genuinely operated at:**
- At Google, his xGE comms work reached and served an internal community of 1,000+ senior individual contributors, and a separate program touched all 75,000+ employees.
- In newsrooms, he led a team of 10+ producers at AJ+ and coached three of them into on-camera principal roles that went on to win industry recognition.
- His written and produced work has reached tens of millions of people in a single piece.

**The honest limit:** at Google, Mitchell was a senior individual contributor, not a people manager. His xGE and Corp Eng roles were program and communications leadership, not a formal management chain with direct reports. His clearest people-management evidence (leading and developing a team of producers) is from the newsroom era, not the most recent chapter.

**For the assistant:** when asked about seniority or scale, cite the real numbers above without inflating "led" or "influenced" into "managed."
