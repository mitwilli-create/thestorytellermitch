The honest version

This network is broadcast-heavy, built across national TV and radio, with a real tier-one AI and business cluster on top: named relationships at the New York Times (including a lead AI columnist), Bloomberg, CNBC, ProPublica, and the Journal. It is lighter on the pure data-center trade press that covers Fluidstack most directly. So the honest pitch is the strong one: I hold live relationships with the reporters who set the national and AI narrative, and I know exactly how to build the trade-press layer fast. Every relationship is scored by warmth and beat, so outreach starts where it is warmest, not cold.

The newsroom record behind the network is on the record too: the Scientology coverage, the Ramos newsroom under a $500M lawsuit, and the judgment record on the comms page.

Names and contacts are deliberately withheld here. A rolodex is worth something precisely because it is not public; I open specific relationships once there is a signed reason to, not before. That discipline is part of the job.
