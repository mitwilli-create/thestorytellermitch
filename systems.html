How this site is made and checked

Systems and checks.

This site ships through the discipline it argues for. Every change passes a fixed set of gates before deploy, each gate is a script, and the record below is what the gates measured: dated, with the one open flag listed rather than hidden.

The standing gates, every deploy

  • Definition auditA headless browser walks every page at two viewports and compares each rendered image, poster, and loop against its intrinsic pixels. Anything under 1.6x its rendered size gets re-sourced from a better master or logged as a known ceiling.
  • Bake driftGenerated regions (the reel, the case files, the writing index) must match their generators exactly. Hand edits inside baked markup fail the build.
  • Loop seamsEvery ambient loop is a measured self-crossfade: seam scored at the cycle boundary, palette checked against the approved still, motion compared to the accepted predecessor, and a frame montage reviewed by eye before anything ships.
  • LoudnessAudio-bearing clips are measured to EBU R128 and normalized to -16 LUFS with true peaks held at -1.5 dBTP.
  • PerformanceLargest contentful paint under 2.5 seconds and zero layout shift, held at 1440 and 390 wide, measured under a throttled harness and again on the live site after deploy.
  • AccessibilityKeyboard, touch, reduced-motion, and focus behavior are exercised on every playable surface, not assumed from markup.
  • Code reviewEvery commit set goes through automated review and doesn't ship until majors and regressions read zero.
  • Outside eyesEach shipped round of changes is reviewed by a seven-model panel, and its verdict is logged before the next round starts.
  • Edge freshnessAfter deploy, both hosts are polled until the new bytes actually serve. The CDN's word isn't taken for it.
